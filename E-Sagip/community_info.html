Disaster Preparedness Bulletin

Flood Preparedness

1 Keep emergency bags ready (ID, medicines, valuables, 3 days of food/water)
2 Know your evacuation route before floodwaters rise
3 Turn off electrical appliances when flooding is imminent
4 Never walk through moving floodwater — it can knock you down
5 Stay informed via PAGASA and Barangay announcements

Typhoon Safety

1 Reinforce windows and doors before the typhoon arrives
2 Secure loose outdoor items that could become projectiles
3 Charge all devices and prepare flashlights with spare batteries
4 Fill clean containers with water before the storm
5 Stay indoors during the typhoon eye — it will pass, then resume

Fire Emergency

1 Do not use elevators — always take the stairs during a fire
2 Crawl low under smoke to avoid inhalation
3 Call 911 or BFP immediately — do not attempt to fight large fires
4 Touch doors before opening — if hot, use another exit
5 Have a designated family meeting point outside

Donation Guidelines

1 Coordinate with Barangay Hall before donating to avoid duplication
2 Bring non-perishable food items (canned goods, instant noodles, rice)
3 Hygiene kits (soap, toothbrush, sanitary pads) are always needed
4 Clothing donations should be clean and usable
5 Cash donations go directly to BDRRMC relief operations fund
